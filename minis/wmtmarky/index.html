The top 8000 students in BC (equal to the top ~13% of around 60 000 Grade 12s) are awarded a $1250 scholarship voucher from the BC government. This is determined based on the BC Achievement Average, which is different from a student's overall GPA. Only the courses used to meet graduation requirements are considered, with a student's English Studies 12 mark used in the event of a tie.
